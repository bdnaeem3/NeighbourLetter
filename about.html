About Us

Our Story

At Neighbor Letters, we believe that there is a better way to sell your house or land. A way where you spend much less time, that takes almost no effort and sells your house or land in the shortest amount of time. We’ve very passionate about it, and our mission is to help every owner to sell their house or land fast without calling an agent, paying huge fees, listing it, or convincing anyone that it's in a great area.

We focus on selling your house or vacant land to your neighbors or cash buyers in your area because of one simple reason - people who already own real estate in the neighborhood don’t need much convincing about the area’s fabulousness.

You may have tried some other ways to sell your house or land, for example, hiring a Virtual Assistant (VA) as hundreds of our customers did before they started using Neighbor Letters.

However, do any of these hardships sound familiar to you, when it comes to a VA?

  • It takes about 1/2 a day to get back the neighbor addresses and maps for your property

  • They do not mail it for you (as they are usually not based in the US).

  • They’re usually not on time, and they are not aware of which neighbor or which property is calling - an absolute customer service mess.

  • Your work may not get prioritized against other people using the same VA, so your expectations on time management are not met

If you really value your time and don’t want to waste it, Neighbor Letters is the best way for you to have your property seen fast. As you see, hiring a VA just adds more load on your shoulders you don’t need as managing people takes time!

Or let’s look at one more way; which is sending neighbor letters by yourself. We asked our customers who used to do it themselves, and this is what they said:

“I hate it. No one I know enjoys doing it one bit. Even after I have a VA do it all for me, there is still work. It doesn't end or go away.”

“Each letter takes 15-30 minutes, depending on the workload. The more successful you are, the more it hurts to do this sort of thing.”

“Takes 1-2 days, which is a long time that I don’t have to spare.”

Do these sound familiar to you? If yes, Neighbor Letters will take you out of this misery for good.

We’re excited to simplify the house or land selling and marketing process for YOU and everyone through our wonderful service: Neighbor Letters.
